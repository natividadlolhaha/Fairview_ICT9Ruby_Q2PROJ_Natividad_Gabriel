Taho
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup soy milk - unsweetened (5 grams fat per 100 ml)
  • ½ tablespoon cornstarch
  • ¼ - 1 teaspoon Epsom salt
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • ⅛ cup tapioca pearls (small) - uncooked
Steps:
  1. Boil water in a kettle then set aside
  2. Prepare a pot and place it on top of the stove. Place a heat-proof bowl big enough for 500ml of liquid in the middle of the pot.
  3. In a cup, dissolve cornstarch in 1 tablespoon of water, then stir in Epsom salt until dissolved.
  4. Pour the Epsom salt mixture into the bowl, then slowly add the soy milk
  5. Pour the boiling water into the pot, just below the level of the soy milk mixture.
  6. Cover the pot with a lid wrapped in a cloth. Simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes until the Taho is firm but wiggly.
  7. Boil equal parts of brown sugar and water, simmer for 7-10 minutes until thickened.
  8. Boil water, add pearls, cook for 10 minutes, strain, rinse, then cook again until translucent.
  9. Put your taho in a glass and enjoy!
