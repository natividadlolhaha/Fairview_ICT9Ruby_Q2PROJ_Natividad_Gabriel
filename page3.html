Sisig
Ingredients:
  • 1 lb pork belly, boiled and chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 red chili peppers, chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp calamansi juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Boil the pork belly until tender, then chop it into small pieces.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© garlic, onion, and chili peppers.
  3. Add the boiled pork and stir-fry until crispy.
  4. Season with soy sauce, vinegar, calamansi juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Transfer to a sizzling plate and optionally top with a raw egg.
  6. Serve with rice and enjoy!
